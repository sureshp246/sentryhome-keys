Overview

SentryHome is a self-hosted Tesla Sentry Mode monitoring application. Your data is processed and stored on your own hardware — not on our servers. We have no access to your Tesla account, vehicle data, or personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what data SentryHome collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. By using SentryHome, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

SentryHome is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Tesla, Inc.

// 02

Data We Collect

Because SentryHome is self-hosted, data is stored on your own server. The app and backend collect only what is necessary to function:

  • Tesla OAuth tokens (encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM on your server)
  • Vehicle identifier (VIN) to associate events with your vehicle
  • Sentry Mode event metadata (event type, camera name, timestamp)
  • Apple Push Notification device tokens (stored encrypted, used only to deliver alerts)
  • App usage data for emergency kill switch and audit trail purposes
  • Security audit log entries (privacy-preserving — see Section 5)

// 03

Data We Never Collect

SentryHome is designed with privacy by default. The following data is never collected, logged, or stored:

  • Tesla account passwords or login credentials
  • Raw OAuth tokens or refresh tokens in plaintext
  • Exact GPS coordinates or precise location data (off by default)
  • Full raw telemetry payloads long-term
  • APNs device tokens in plaintext
  • Private keys or signing keys
  • Camera footage or video clips
  • Personal communications
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking data

// 04

How Data Is Used

Data collected by SentryHome is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating with Tesla's Fleet API on your behalf
  • Receiving and processing Sentry Mode events from your vehicle
  • Delivering push notifications to your registered iOS devices
  • Enabling vehicle commands (honk, flash, lock) when explicitly enabled by you
  • Maintaining a tamper-evident security audit log
  • Performing encrypted daily backups to your designated storage

Your data is never sold, shared, rented, or disclosed to any third party. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the operation of the application.

// 05

Data Storage & Security

SentryHome implements multiple layers of security to protect your data:

  • Full disk encryption (LUKS) on the home server
  • AES-256-GCM application-level encryption for all Tesla tokens
  • Envelope encryption (KEK/DEK) — per-user encryption keys, master key stored separately
  • SOPS + age for secrets management — no plaintext secrets on disk
  • mTLS between your Tesla vehicle and the telemetry ingress server
  • WireGuard encrypted tunnel between cloud ingress and home server
  • tesla-signer service isolated with no database access and restricted internet egress
  • Command service disabled by default — requires explicit user activation
  • Generic APNs push payloads — no sensitive event details in notification payload
  • Append-only audit log with SHA-256 hash chain integrity

The audit log records security-relevant events (login, command requests, token rotations) but never contains raw tokens, exact coordinates, APNs tokens in plaintext, or full telemetry payloads.

// 06

Data Retention

SentryHome enforces automatic data retention policies:

  • Raw telemetry data: automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Normalized Sentry events: automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Audit log entries: automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Encrypted backups: retained for 30 days then purged
  • Location data: disabled by default; purged immediately when disabled

You can request immediate deletion of all your data at any time using the emergency controls within the application.

Third Parties

SentryHome interacts with the following third-party services as part of its operation:

  • Tesla, Inc. — Vehicle telemetry and command API. Subject to Tesla's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Inc. — Push Notification Service (APNs) for delivering alerts to iOS devices. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare — DNS, DDoS protection, and encrypted backup storage (R2). Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Hetzner — VPS hosting for the telemetry ingress node only. No user data is stored on this server. Subject to Hetzner's Privacy Policy.

No other third parties receive your data. We do not use analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Tesla API Usage

SentryHome uses Tesla's official Fleet API and Fleet Telemetry protocol. When you connect your Tesla account:

  • You authenticate directly with Tesla via OAuth — your credentials go to Tesla, not to us
  • We receive an OAuth refresh token which is immediately encrypted and stored only on your server
  • Access tokens are short-lived and refreshed automatically
  • You can revoke SentryHome's access at any time from your Tesla account settings
  • SentryHome requests only the permissions necessary for Sentry monitoring and optional vehicle commands

SentryHome is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official partner of Tesla, Inc. Tesla and Sentry Mode are trademarks of Tesla, Inc.

// 09

Push Notifications

SentryHome uses Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) to deliver Sentry Mode alerts to your iPhone. By design:

  • Push notification payloads are generic — they contain no sensitive event details
  • The payload only signals that a new event is available
  • Event details are fetched directly from your home server only after you unlock your phone
  • This prevents sensitive vehicle information from appearing on your lock screen
  • APNs device tokens are stored encrypted on your server and never transmitted to third parties

Your Rights

Because SentryHome is self-hosted, you have full control over your data at all times. You can:

  • Access all data stored about you directly from your PostgreSQL database
  • Delete your account and all associated data using the emergency kill switches
  • Revoke Tesla OAuth access from your Tesla account settings page
  • Export your Sentry event history at any time
  • Disable location data collection at any time
  • Choose your privacy mode (Standard, Enhanced, or Maximum Privacy)
  • Rotate your encryption keys at any time
  • Remove a specific device from receiving notifications
