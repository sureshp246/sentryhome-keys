// capabilities

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Instant Alerts

Fleet Telemetry pushes events directly from your Tesla. No polling. No delays. Real-time Sentry Mode notifications the moment something happens.

Privacy First

Your Tesla data flows through your own server — not ours, not anyone else's. Tokens encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Private keys never leave your machine.

Sentry Monitoring

Know the moment anyone approaches your car. Proximity alerts, alarm state, arming status — all visible in real time on your iPhone.

Vehicle Commands

Lock, unlock, honk — all from your iPhone. Commands signed with your private key via Tesla's Virtual Key system.

Live Vehicle Status

Battery level, charging state, lock status — all updated in real time via Fleet Telemetry without waking your sleeping car.

Event History

Full timeline of sentry events with configurable retention. Standard, Enhanced, or Stealth privacy mode — you choose how much is kept.

// how it works

Four steps.
Total peace of mind.

01

Sentry Detects

Your Tesla's Sentry Mode spots activity around your vehicle — someone approaching, touching, or triggering an alarm.

02

You're Notified

An instant push notification arrives on your iPhone. No delays, no polling, no missed alerts.

03

You Review

Open Sentri to see exactly what happened — event type, severity, and a full timeline of activity.

04

You Respond

Honk the horn, lock the car — all from your iPhone, anywhere in the world. Instantly.

All privately. All from your phone. Your data never leaves your control.

// privacy by design

Your data is never
sold or shared.

No data selling

Your sentry events, tokens, and history are never sold, shared, or monetized. Zero third-party access, zero advertising.

Encrypted at rest

Tesla credentials, VINs, and APNs tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Nothing sensitive is ever stored in plaintext.

No location tracking

Sentri never collects your vehicle's GPS location or driving data. Off by default, always.

You're in control

Delete your data anytime. Revoke Tesla access instantly. Choose your event retention window. Your account, your rules.
